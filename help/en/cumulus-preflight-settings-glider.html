Preflight Settings

Glider

Here you can select the glider to be used for your flight.

Glider selection window
In this window you can select one of the gliders defined in the Glider tabulator of Cumulus Settings. The glider is listed with its tpye, registration and competition sign.
Deselect button
Pressing the button will deselect the current selected glider.
Co-Pilot
Name of the Co-pilot, if the gilder is a double seater.
Added Load
Here you can define the added load, e.g. weight of the pilot, etc.
Water balast
Here you can define the water balast, filled in in your glider before take off.
