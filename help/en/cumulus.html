Cumulus


  Cumulus is a program aimed at the soaring community. It provides a moving map display with all the information you need to easier navigate while flying, yet requiring a minimum of user-interaction. Navigation during flight is based on processing of standard NMEA records, delivered by a GPS receiver.

Cumulus features a full-color moving map with information on terrain, airfields, airspace, (rail-) roads, water, cities and many other details. It handles waypoints, igc-logging and tasks in cooperation with KFLog. Wind, reachable sites, final glide, sun rise and sun set calculation and automatic task point switch are also supported.
